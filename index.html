Droupadi Murmu

Droupadi Murmu is the current and 15th President of India. He assumed office on July 25, 2023.

Prior to his presidency,Droupadi Murmu served as the Governor of the Indian state of Jharkhand from 2015 to 2021.

In 1997, Droupadi Murmu was elected as the councillor of the Rairangpur Nagar Panchayat as an independent candidate from a reserved seat for women. She then joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP).

Role of the President

The President of India is the head of state and the first citizen of India. The role of the President is largely ceremonial, representing the unity and integrity of the nation.

The President performs various constitutional and ceremonial duties, including the appointment of the Prime Minister, Chief Justice of India, and other high officials.

Powers and Functions

The President of India has several powers and functions, including the power to grant pardons, commute sentences, and issue ordinances.

The President also addresses the nation on various occasions, such as Independence Day and Republic Day.
